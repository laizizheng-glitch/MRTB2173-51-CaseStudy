# Sprint 1 MVP Findings

## Dataset Summary

- Records after exact-duplicate removal: **7,043**
- Columns: **21**
- Historical churn rate: **26.5%**
- Duplicate customer-ID records flagged: **0**

## Main Exploratory Findings

1. The contract group with the highest historical churn rate is
   **Month-to-month**
   at **42.7%**.
2. The tenure group with the highest historical churn rate is
   **0-12 months**
   at **47.4%**.
3. The payment-method group with the highest historical churn rate is
   **Electronic check**
   at **45.3%**.
